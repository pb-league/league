THREE WAYS TO CROWN A CHAMPION

Every format handles seeding, byes, and partner assignment automatically — including for odd player counts.

Single Elimination

Classic bracket. One loss and you're out. Fastest format, perfect for end-of-season finales.

  • Top seeds vs bottom seeds
  • Top seed gets bye if odd count
  • Fixed doubles teams throughout
  • Champion when one team remains
Most Popular

Double Elimination

Two separate brackets — winners and losers. Players need two losses to be eliminated, giving everyone a second chance.

  • Winners & Losers brackets
  • Grand Final: best of both brackets
  • Live status: who's in which bracket
  • Automatic loser bracket routing

Round Robin Reseeded

Everyone plays every round unless buys are necessary. No eliminations — the player with the most wins at the end takes the title.

  • Partners re-pair each round
  • Bye rotates to weakest player
  • Live standings after every round
  • Swiss-system fairness

01

Hundreds of Random Starts

Each optimization run evaluates up to 500 independently shuffled candidate solutions, guaranteeing broad coverage of the solution space.

Can rerun as many times as admin wants and it will only save if it finds a better solution.

02

Automatic Weight Calibration

Weights are normalized by sampling real distributions, so a "50" for partner repetition and "15" for rank balance are genuinely proportional — not just raw numbers.

03

Season-Aware History

Every partner and opponent pairing from the full season is tracked. The optimizer actively avoids rematches across weeks, not just within a single session.

04

Rank-Based Balance

Player rankings feed directly into pairing decisions. Initial rankings let the optimizer balance games even before season standings exist.

Role-Based Access

SIX ROLES.
RIGHT LEVEL FOR EVERYONE.

Every person in the league gets exactly the access they need — nothing more, nothing less. Roles are assigned per-player with a single dropdown.

★
App Manager
Developer / platform owner
Full platform control. Can manage all leagues, configure any setting, and set limits on what league admins can do. Identified by a secure password stored outside the spreadsheet — inaccessible to anyone else. Logs in via a dedicated protected path.
⚙️
League Admin
League organizer
Full control of their league. Manages players and PINs, configures the schedule and weights, generates pairings, runs tournaments, enters and deletes scores, approves registrations, and sends all emails. Logs in with a separate admin password — never confused with a player PIN.
🤝
Admin Assistant
Trusted volunteer
Can run the league on nights the admin is unavailable. Generates and locks pairings, runs tournaments, marks attendance, and enters new scores — but cannot view player PINs or emails, cannot delete or overwrite existing scores, and cannot clear pairings. Perfect for a trusted regular player.
✏️
Scorer
Score entry only
Has everything a regular player sees, plus the ability to enter scores from the player dashboard. Ideal for court captains who record results as games finish. Spectator-role members can also be granted scoring access — useful for large tournament helpers who don't play.
👀
Spectator
Non-playing member
Follows the league without playing — never appears in pairings or standings. Can view all scores, standings, and brackets. Optionally granted score-entry access, making them ideal as neutral scorekeepers at tournaments.
🎾
Player
Default for all members
View pairings, scores, standings, ranking trends, and tournament brackets. On the final round, see a live scenario analysis showing which results you need to finish in the top 3. Fast two-phase load works on any phone or tablet. Supports creating an icon on the phone to make it work just like an app. Can self-register with an invite code pending admin approval.
🔐
Separate Admin Authentication
Admins log in with a dedicated password — completely separate from the player PIN system. No player can accidentally gain admin access by choosing the same PIN. The App Manager password is stored in the server environment, never in any spreadsheet.
